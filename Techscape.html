Techscape

Mobile game made using Unity (C#)

This project is issued by the Colruyt group, a Belgian supermarket chain, to teach people the different types of mechanical difficulties that take place in stores.

Prototyping (20 Sept-Present)

We started by testing out how to work on mobile games and setting up a few camera setups and testing from what perspective we would be working for the rest of the project. Furthermore we gathered some reference for puzzle ideas and discussed what we envisioned with the client. The next week I created some prototypes of how some puzzles could work and how to interact with the puzzles. Finally in the last week of prototyping, I worked on UI and creating a vertical slice of what the game will entail.

Prototype

The puzzle moves closer to the camera and the player can click on the cylinders to rotate them and disconnect its flow(red).
