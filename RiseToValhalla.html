Rise to Valhalla

Arcade game for PC made using Unity (C#)

Summary

Rise To Valhalla is a fastpaced beat-em-up platformer in which the goal is to try and get all the way up to Valhalla. However, valkyries are blocking the way up and the tide of hell is chasing you to drag you down to hell forever. The higher you get, the higher your score. Slay the valkyries to increase your score and use their wings to climb even higher. Challenge your friends to climb higher than you and beat your highscore.

My contributions

Patrolling enemies (grounded)
Player teleportation (slowing, moving)
Player and grounded enemy attacks (default,slowing,rooting)
